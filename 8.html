SANTOSA ISLAND

The best time to visit is the early evening and the visitor can get a view of both day and night. The park is open from 09:30 till 22:00 (23:00 on weekends). The observation deck may be closed on certain days, kindly check the website before planning a visit.

BEST TIME TO VISIT

During the month of December and January, the temperatures and the humidity is lowest, with the average Sentosa temperature. While rains become fairly consistent at 4-13 times a month, they usually take place during the early mornings. This is the best time to visit Sentosa.

CLIMATE

The island is located just one degree north of the equator and has a tropical rainforest climate, making it humid, rainy and hot throughout much of the year.

LOCAL FOOD

BIRD PARADISE

Largest and most diverse bird park in Asia: Bird Paradise Singapore is home to over 3500 birds from more than 400 species, making it the largest and most diverse bird park in Asia. Interactive experience: You can enjoy interactive experiences with the birds such as feeding and bird shows.

LOCATION

Bird Paradise is an aviary located in Mandai, Singapore. Opened on 8 May 2023, it replaced the Jurong Bird Park and forms a part of the Mandai Wildlife Reserve, consisting of the Singapore Zoo, Night Safari and River Wonders and the upcoming Rainforest Wild Park. Bird Paradise covers approximately 17 hectares (42 acres), with larger and double the walk-in aviaries than the previous Jurong Bird Park.

CLIMATE

Mandalay weather enjoys a semi-tropical climate, which characterized by warm to hot summer and mild to cold winters. The average temperature is always high, about 25 oC in the summer and 18 oC in the winter.The city's yearly temperature is 28.1ºC (82.58ºF) and it is 1.08% higher than Myanmar's averages. Mandalay typically receives about 78.25 millimeters (3.08 inches) of precipitation and has 149.9 rainy days (41.07% of the time) annually.

BEST TIME TO VISIT

The best time to visit Bird Paradise Singapore is in the morning or late afternoon when the birds are most active. Weekdays tend to be less crowded than weekends and public holidays, providing with a more tranquil experience.

MARINA BAY SANDE

Marina Bay Sands is an integrated resort fronting Marina Bay in Singapore and a landmark of the city. At its opening in 2010, it was deemed the world's most expensive standalone casino property at S$8 billion (US$6.88 billion).[2][3] The resort includes a 2,561-room hotel, a 120,000-square-metre (1,300,000 sq ft) convention-exhibition centre.

LOCATION

Marina Bay is a bay located in the Central Area of Singapore, surrounded by the perimeter of four other planning areas, the Downtown Core, Marina East, Marina South and Straits View.

CLIMATE

Singapore is situated near the equator and has a tropical climate, with relatively high and uniform temperatures, abundant rainfall, and high humidity throughout the year. The climate of Singapore is characterized by two monsoon seasons separated by inter-monsoonal periods.
